About the Series

Set in 1978 Memphis but brought to life in 2025, Black Monarch Records follows James Bell and his crew as they navigate the highs and horrors of running a Black-owned soul label. Influenced by real history and powered by AI, this micro-series blends vintage grit with next-gen visuals.
